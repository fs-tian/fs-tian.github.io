Biography

I'm a PhD student of the School of Computer Science and Engineering at South China University of Technology. I got my M.S. in the School of Computer, Electronics and Information at Guangxi University in 2022, advised by Prof. Junbin Liang.

Research Interests

My research interests include edge computing, machine learning and edge intelligence. In particular, I am interested in federated learning, distributed algorithms, network optimization, resource scheduling, and game theory.
